Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about the Evalvate platform and how it works.

Our AI simulates a comprehensive interview experience. It analyzes what you say (clarity, relevance) and how you say it (vocal tone, confidence, filler words), along with your non-verbal cues (facial expressions, engagement). It then provides a detailed feedback report to help you master every aspect of your communication.

Our platform is launching with a focus on technical and business roles to ensure the highest quality feedback. Our roadmap includes rapid expansion into a wide range of non-technical fields, including marketing, sales, and creative industries.

Absolutely. You can upload your resume or manually input your skills, desired role, and target industry. Our AI uses this data to generate a customized interview experience with questions and challenges relevant to you.

To experience the full power of our multimodal analysis, a working webcam and microphone are required. For best results, we recommend a quiet, well-lit environment to ensure the AI can accurately analyze your delivery.

We take your privacy seriously. All interview data is encrypted and stored securely. You have full control over your recordings and can delete them at any time. We will never share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent.

Our AI is trained on a vast and diverse dataset of professional interviews and communication patterns, developed in consultation with industry experts and recruiters. This ensures the feedback you receive is relevant, accurate, and aligned with what top companies are looking for.
